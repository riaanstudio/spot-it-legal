Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

Spot It is published by Riaan Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

1. Information We Collect

Spot It does not require user registration and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other direct personal details inside the app.

The app stores gameplay progress and best scores locally on your device so levels can stay unlocked and your best score can be shown in the app.

2. Advertising

Spot It displays banner ads using Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect limited device and usage information, such as advertising identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, and ad interaction data, to serve and improve ads.

For more information, please review Google's policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Storage

Gameplay-related data such as unlocked levels and best scores is stored on the device using app preferences. Riaan Studio does not operate a user account system or public profile system for this app.

4. Permissions and Network Access

Spot It uses internet and network state access to load banner ads. The app may also use vibration for gameplay feedback.

5. Children

Spot It is a simple casual game. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information directly to us, please contact us and we will review the concern.

6. Third-Party Services

The app currently uses Google AdMob for banner advertising. Third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies and practices.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Riaan Studio at riaanstudio@gmail.com.
